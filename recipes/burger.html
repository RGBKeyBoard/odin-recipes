Burger


What is it?


A burger is a type of food. It is made of a bun with a patty and other ingredients inside.


Ingredients

  • Bun, the bread that holds the burger
  • Meat, such as beef or pork
  • Cheese, probably something like american
  • Vegetables, such as lettuce or tomato if you are weird

Steps

  1. Place the bun in a baking dish
  2. Place the meat patty on the bun
  3. Layer the cheese on top
