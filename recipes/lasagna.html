Lasagna


What is it?


Lasagna is a type of pasta. It is made of sheets of pasta layered with other ingredients and baked.


Ingredients

  • Pasta noodle, the noodle that looks like a sheet
  • Meat, such as beef or pork
  • Cheese, such as mozzarella or parmesan
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Place the pasta sheets in a baking dish
  2. Spread the meat sauce over the pasta
  3. Layer the cheese on top
  4. Bake in the oven until golden brown
